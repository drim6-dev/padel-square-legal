# Privacy Policy for Padel Square Marrakech

**Last Updated:** November 3, 2025

## Introduction

Padel Square Marrakech ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Padel Square Marrakech mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use our App, we may collect:

- **Phone Number**: Used for account creation and authentication via SMS OTP
- **Full Name**: To identify you within the app and to other users
- **Username**: Your unique identifier in the app
- **Profile Picture** (optional): Avatar image you upload
- **Skill Level**: Your padel playing level (1-7)
- **Location Data**: Your device's location to show nearby courts and relevant content

### 2. Information Automatically Collected

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the App, including booking history, friends list, and chat activity
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Push Notification Token**: To send you notifications about bookings, messages, and friend requests

### 3. Information from Your Activity

- **Court Bookings**: Dates, times, courts booked, and booking preferences
- **Messages**: Chat messages you send to other users (text and images)
- **Friend Connections**: Your friend list and friend requests
- **Wallet Balance**: Your current balance for court bookings (recharged on-site at our facility)
- **Transaction History**: Record of bookings, cancellations, and refunds

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and Maintain the Service**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Process court bookings and cancellations
   - Manage your wallet balance and on-site recharges
   - Facilitate split payment requests with friends

2. **Communication**
   - Send booking confirmations and reminders
   - Send push notifications for messages, friend requests, and booking updates
   - Respond to your inquiries and support requests

3. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve features
   - Monitor and analyze trends
   - Detect and prevent technical issues

4. **Matchmaking and Social Features**
   - Connect you with other players of similar skill level
   - Show available players for booking sessions
   - Enable friend connections and chat messaging

5. **Location-Based Services**
   - Show you the nearest courts
   - Provide relevant court availability
   - Enhance booking experience with location context

---

## How We Share Your Information

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### With Other Users
- **Public Profile**: Your name, username, avatar, and skill level are visible to other users
- **Friends**: Your friends can see your booking activity and availability
- **Chat Messages**: Messages you send are visible to the recipient

### With Service Providers
We use third-party services to operate our App:

1. **Supabase** (Database and Authentication)
   - Stores your account data, bookings, and messages
   - Located in: Cloud infrastructure
   - Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

2. **Expo Push Notification Service**
   - Delivers push notifications to your device
   - Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

3. **Google Firebase Cloud Messaging** (Android notifications)
   - Delivers push notifications to Android devices
   - Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. **Apple Push Notification Service** (iOS notifications, when available)
   - Delivers push notifications to iOS devices
   - Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### For Legal Reasons
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Messages**: Stored indefinitely unless you delete them
- **Booking History**: Retained for 2 years for record-keeping
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Update
You can access and update your personal information through the App's Profile settings.

### Delete Account
You can request account deletion by contacting us. We will delete your data within 30 days.

### Location Data
You can disable location access in your device settings, but some features may not work properly.

### Push Notifications
You can disable push notifications in your device settings or within the App.

### Marketing Communications
We do not send marketing communications at this time. If we do in the future, you will be able to opt out.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication**: Phone number verification via SMS OTP
- **Access Control**: Row-level security policies in our database
- **Regular Updates**: We regularly update our security practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
